Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Eggs 1-2
Butter Enough to spread over the entire pan
Milk 2 tablespoons (largely personal preference)
Salt 1 tablespoon (depends on serving size)
Pepper 1 tablespoon (depends on serving size)
(Optional Replacement for Milk) Water 2 tablespoons (largely personal preference)

Instructions

  1. Take eggs out of fridge
  2. Crack eggs into medium bowl
  3. Beat eggs, salt, and pepper
    1. Beat the eggs first, then add in the salt and pepper and beat the entire mixture
  4. Turn on pan and spread butter
    1. Spread butter over entire pan (i.e. with a spatula)
  5. Pour mixture in once butter starts to bubble
  6. As eggs begin to set, pull mixture across pan to form large curds
  7. Continue pulling, lifting, and folding until no more liquid eggs remain
  8. Remove the eggs from the pan and serve

Optimal Steps for Optimal Steak

  1. Don't use a cast iron pan
  2. Don't overcook. The eggs will continue to cook even after taken off heat
  3. Substitute milk and butter with cooking spray and water to lighten the egs up
